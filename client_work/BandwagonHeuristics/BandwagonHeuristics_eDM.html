Don't let tensions boil over
We've been cooking up a storm around Constructive Challenges these past few weeks and really getting to the meat of building team trust. We hope that you and your team have found these tast and added them to your recipe for great communication in the workplace.
Raise
If something doesn't feel right, speak up and challenge constuctively. Spicy opinions can help up have better conversations.
Respond
Not every challenge will be to your taste but showing appreaciation for challenges acknowledges your team's input and leads to high trust relationships.
Reassure
Create a safe space and encourage your team to express their views confidently. Open up discussions so everyone feels heard.
